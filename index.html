Sparrow

Sparrows are small, brown-grey birds commonly found near human habitats. They are social and chirpy.

Peacock

The peacock is known for its colorful feathers and beautiful dance. It is the national bird of India.

Parrot

Parrots are intelligent birds known for their ability to mimic human speech and bright green color.
